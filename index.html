What I Learned

Discovering the Power of HTML5

HTML5 amazed me with its simplicity and power. Using semantic tags like <header>, <nav>, and <article> helped me build a page that is both easy to read and easy to navigate—not just for people but also for search engines.

Why Accessibility Matters to Me

Learning about accessibility opened my eyes to the importance of designing websites that everyone can use. I want my work to break barriers, helping users with disabilities have the same smooth experience as everyone else.
